FAQ

  • Why does the game need Photos access? TimeStamp! uses photo library metadata to generate date-guessing rounds.
  • Can I play without photo access? Core gameplay requires photo access to create rounds from your images.
  • How do I report a bug? Email us with your device model, iOS version, and steps to reproduce.
