Privacy Policy for TimeStamp!

Effective date: March 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Maxwell Games handles information when you use TimeStamp!.

Information We Access

  • Photo Library Data: Used to generate gameplay rounds based on your photos and dates.
  • Game Data: Scores, settings, and gameplay progress.
  • Identifiers and Diagnostics: May be processed by integrated services (for example, Firebase and Apple Game Center) for app functionality and stability.

How We Use Information

  • Provide core gameplay features.
  • Enable leaderboards and social gameplay features.
  • Improve app performance, reliability, and support.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data may be processed by service providers required to operate the app, such as analytics, backend, and platform services.

Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary for app functionality, legal compliance, and support operations.

Your Choices

  • You can revoke Photos permission at any time in iOS Settings.
  • You can request data-deletion support by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

TimeStamp! is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child provided personal information, contact us to request review and removal where applicable.
