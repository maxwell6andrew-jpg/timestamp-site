Data Deletion

Andrew Maxwell, d/b/a Maxwell Games  ·  support@timestampgame.com

You have the right to request deletion of personal data we hold about you — including under GDPR (EEA/UK users) and CCPA (California users). We process all eligible deletion requests within 30 days.

How to Request Deletion
1
Send an email to support
Email support@timestampgame.com with the subject line exactly as written:

Data Deletion Request
2
Include your identifier
Include any identifier associated with your account — your Apple Game Center display name, or the email linked to your Game Center account. This helps us locate your data accurately. If you're unsure, just describe how you used the app and we'll help.
3
Confirm your identity
We may ask a follow-up question to verify you are the account holder before processing. This is to protect your data from unauthorized deletion requests.
4
We process and confirm
We'll acknowledge your request within 3 business days and complete deletion within 30 days. You'll receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete.
What Gets Deleted
🗑️
Game scores & history
All daily challenge scores, leaderboard entries, and gameplay history stored in our systems.
🗑️
Account identifiers
Any Game Center Player IDs or device identifiers linked to your account in Firebase.
🗑️
Usage & analytics data
Aggregate usage data associated with your account in Firebase Analytics.
🗑️
Support correspondence
Emails sent to our support address, upon explicit request.
What May Be Retained

Some data may be retained where required by law or for legitimate operational reasons:

📋
Legal obligations
Data we are required to keep under applicable law, tax, or regulatory requirements.
🔒
Fraud & abuse records
Records related to violations of our Terms of Use, retained for security purposes.
📊
Anonymized analytics
Aggregate, non-identifiable analytics data that cannot be linked back to you individually.
📷
Your photos
Your photos are never uploaded to our servers and remain entirely on your device.
Ready to submit a request?

Email us directly with subject line "Data Deletion Request" and we'll take care of the rest. We respond within 3 business days and complete all eligible requests within 30 days.

✉️   Email us now
Or write to: support@timestampgame.com
Additional Information

If you are located in the European Economic Area or United Kingdom, you may also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority if you believe your rights have not been respected.

California residents may also exercise rights under the CCPA including the right to know, right to correct, and right to non-discrimination. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Note: Deleting your data from our systems does not delete your Apple Game Center account or Game Center leaderboard history, which is managed separately by Apple. To manage Game Center data, visit Settings → Game Center on your device or contact Apple Support.
