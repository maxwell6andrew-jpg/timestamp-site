Data Deletion Requests

If you want data associated with your use of TimeStamp! reviewed or deleted, contact us using the details below.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Email andrewmaxwellbusiness@gmail.com with subject line: Data Deletion Request.
  2. Include the username or identifier used in the app (if available).
  3. Include your request details and confirmation that you are the account owner.

Processing Timeline

We typically respond within 1-3 business days and complete eligible deletion requests within 30 days, subject to legal obligations.

Notes

  • Some data may be retained where required by law, security, or fraud-prevention obligations.
  • Device-level photos remain in your own photo library unless you delete them from your device.
