Features
01
Your photos, your game

TimeStamp uses photos from your own library. Every round is personal — no stock images, no shared pools. Just your memories.

02
Progressive guessing

Narrow it down step by step — year first, then month, then day. Each layer earns points. Precision pays off.

03
Compete and compare

Daily challenges put you on the global leaderboard. Share your score and see who knows their own memories best.

How It Works

Three steps. A hundred memories.

Step 01
A photo appears

TimeStamp selects a photo from your library — no context, just the image. Do you remember when it was taken?

→
Step 02
Make your guess

Start with the year, then the month, then narrow it to the exact day. Each level unlocks the next. Closer = more points.

→
Step 03
See your score

The reveal shows the real date alongside your guess. Accumulate points across 5 rounds and post your best to the leaderboard.
